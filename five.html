5:00

Poison never made anyone feel so alive.

five: in the morning
i lie on my bed
thinking about
the things you had said
wish you could feel the words that you say
piercing arrows straight to my head

your: lapis eyes i’d always admire
fawning over the ways that they’d stare
but that night you left me in cold blood
they burned a fiery bright flare
ready.
set.
you aimed at my heart
and no hesitation shot me a glare

the: way you’d fake being the sun
had all my seasons revolved around you
you’d be the spring to my cherry blossoms
but your summer would bring them black fumes
you were the warmth settling my heart
but now all that remains are broken parts

now: i’m afraid im running out of time
lived my whole life afraid of heights
and rightfully maybe so
because as soon as i met you
i’m afraid i fell too far
