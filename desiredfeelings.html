Desired Feelings

We want mutual affection from the one person we crave attention.

Emotions don’t disappear
As fast as they appear.
Yet, what is desired?
We see stereotypical love,
But what we want is more than a rose.
We want mutual affection
From the one person we crave attention.

We grow old and decide,
“I want someone by my side,”
So we seek in hope of romance
With whom we want to dance.
To the sound of falling droplets
In a late Summer night.

To be loved, must be nice.
Heard it was a magical feeling.
An explosion of emotions is more precise.
To hear those three words
Must be a feeling beyond explanation.
And hope is not our wild imagination

So where do we start?
To find these desired feelings that keep tugging with all might.
Is what runs through our minds as we try to sleep at night.
Love is complicated nor easily achieved.
But, patience will definitely be the key.
